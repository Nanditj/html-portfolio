The Best Movies According to Thandi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Back To the Future

This is my favourite movie. It involves a science experiment which allows the wrong person to travel to the past instead of the future. Its a great movie for the entire family.

The Truman Show

A movie about a man in search of happiness. He has a sense that the direction of his life is wrong in some way and seeks to find answers.

The Shinning

This movie made me feel chills, it was humorous, scary, exciting, and ahead of its time in every way. A true classic.
